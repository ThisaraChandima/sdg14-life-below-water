Action Impact Simulator

Flip the switches for the ocean-friendly actions you're willing to take. Every action you commit to lowers your dive gauge further into the ocean
See how deep your impact can take you.

Skip Single-Use Plastics

Swap disposable bottles, straws and bags for reusable alternatives for one week, cutting plastic waste before it reaches the ocean.

3 pts

Join a Beach Clean-Up

Spend a few hours removing debris from a shoreline or riverbank before it washes out to sea.

5 pts

Choose Sustainable Seafood

Check labels or apps for certified sustainable seafood to ease pressure on overfished populations.

4 pts

Reduce Water Usage

Shorter showers and fixing household leaks lower runoff that affects coastal ecosystems.

2 pts

Use Reef-Safe Sunscreen

Choose sunscreens free of chemicals linked to coral bleaching before swimming or diving.

2 pts

Support Marine Restoration

Contribute time or funds to organisations restoring mangroves, seagrass or coral reefs.
